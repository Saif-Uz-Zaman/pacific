Steel Structure

Stonehenge is a sister concern of PES and it is known as a trustworthy leading structural steel fabricator. Stonehenge is bona fide to offer its expertise in the fabrication and installation of structural steel and plate works for various industrial and commercial applications.

With a magnificent production capacity and a plant facility Stonehenge has been supplying fabricated structural steel and plate works for major power & desalination plants, petrochemical plants, oil & gas sector, cement plants, steel mills, high-rise building projects as well as other industrial and commercial complexes in abroad and export markets.
