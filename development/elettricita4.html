Lift Service

Elettricita lift services was founded with private capital. With the efforts made in the last couple of years of effort, the team of the company has been established and proven modern thinking team, succeeded in creating a prosperous company. The members of our professional team have proven their skills and professionalism, which determines the best feedback received from each of our clients.

The work of our highly qualified staff and specialists, the company has provided appropriate equipment and tools necessary for the fulfillment of each order. Each project completed by "Elettricita lift service" is characterized by excellent quality, reliability and certainty of execution, as well as impeccable guarantee and warranty support.

Scope of activities

  • The main activity is the installation, maintenance, repair, reconstruction and modernization of elevators, escalators, parking systems and lifting equipment.
  • Additional work is necessary to complete construction of electrical installations in residential and public buildings.

Technical staff

SThe company employs professional technicians, mechanics and fitters experts with corresponding education and training needed to carry out the operations and procedures related to the operation of the company, such as installation, service and maintenance of elevators, escalators, parking systems and lifting equipment building electrical networks and their subsequent maintenance.

Qualification and specialization of managers in the company

Employees holding managerial positions in the company and responsible for the implementation of strategic projects have received special training in the headquarters of the leading manufacturers of lifts and lifting equipment for civil and industrial use. Our staff have visited the headquarters of the world famous companies such as: THYSSEN, KONE, OTIS in Austria and Finland. We also work with the largest Bulgarian company in the industry IZAMET as our staff have undergone special training in their factory.

Thanks to the skills of our employees, we can provide continuous service and maintenance of all common elevators and escalators and in a way that is eligible by the companies manufacturers of the equipment.

Completed projects

The company has installed and maintains elevators, escalators, parking systems and lifting equipment in a number of projects with both public importance and in buildings, public and private property and residential buildings.

Own emergency service

Given our desire to provide our clients full use of purchased equipment, the company maintains a non-stop service unit. This mobile group consisting of qualified technicians is available 24 hours, 7 days a week, so we promptly correct any problem and restore normal operation of the plant.
